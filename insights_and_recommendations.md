# 📊 Key Insights & Business Recommendations

## 🔍 Key Insights

- **Clothing** is the top-performing category in both revenue ($104K) and sales count (1,700+ transactions)
- **Blouse** is the best-selling and highest-revenue product
- **Jewelry** has the highest average review rating (3.86)
- **Young Adults** contribute the most to total revenue
- **79%** of customers are classified as Loyal (more than 15 previous purchases)
- Only **27%** of customers have an active subscription
- **41.91%** of all orders were purchased with a discount
- **Fall** is the highest-revenue season, while **Summer** is the lowest
- **Senior** customers shop most frequently
- **PayPal** is the most popular payment method

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- **Boost Subscriptions** — Promote exclusive benefits for subscribers to increase the 27% adoption rate
- **Customer Loyalty Programs** — Reward repeat buyers to maintain the large Loyal customer base
- **Review Discount Policy** — Balance discount usage (41.91%) with profit margin control
- **Product Positioning** — Highlight Blouse and Jewelry in marketing campaigns as top-selling and top-rated products
- **Targeted Marketing** — Focus efforts on Young Adults and Senior customers as key segments
